# Regional Map & Cohorts (COHRT) — L2 Detailed Requirements

Each requirement traces to exactly one L1 in [L1.md](L1.md). Acceptance criteria are written so a test can be authored without further clarification; user-visible strings are quoted verbatim from source. Requirements state intended behavior; a `**Status:** Not satisfied` line marks requirements the running system does not yet meet (see [known gaps](../GAPS.md)). Conventions, the ID scheme, and the acceptance-test trace-header format are defined in the [capability registry](../README.md).

---

## L2-COHRT-051: Regional aggregation respects eligibility and consent

**Traces to:** L1-COHRT-012

The regional map shall aggregate only participants who completed the journey and consented to map display; no other participant shall influence the display.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

1. Given the mappable set, when `/map` renders, then each region card counts only participants who are eligible (learning completed) and consented to the map.
2. Given a region whose participants are ineligible or non-consenting, when `/map` renders, then that region does not appear at all.
3. Given the page footer, when it renders, then it summarizes "{count} consenting participants across {n} regions."

## L2-COHRT-052: Cohort readiness display

**Traces to:** L1-COHRT-012

Regions shall be flagged as ready when they reach the cohort threshold of 5 eligible participants.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

1. Given a region with at least 5 eligible, consenting participants, when its card renders, then it carries the badge "Ready to gather".
2. Given a region below the threshold, when its card renders, then it shows "{remaining} seats remaining".

## L2-COHRT-053: Map page rendering

**Traces to:** L1-COHRT-012

`/map` shall render region cards for every qualifying region, with role-appropriate navigation and an explanatory empty state.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

1. Given a signed-in participant or facilitator, when `/map` loads, then the page titled "Regional map & cohorts" lists a card per region with its city, province, and eligible participant count, under the viewer's role-appropriate navigation.
2. Given no qualifying regions, when `/map` loads, then the empty state "No cohort in your region yet" is shown.
