Reconciliation Through Relationships

Design system & component library

Every component used across the RTR Matching Portal mocks, in one place. The direction is “two strands, one circle” — warm, land-grounded, and built for readability first.

On this page: Principles · Color · Typography · Brand & motifs · Buttons · Forms · Tags & status · Avatars · Cards · Cohort circle · Progress · Navigation · Alerts · Tables · Messages · Dialogs · Empty states · Accessibility

Principles

Welcome before wow

Participants range widely in age and comfort with technology. Large type, plain words, one clear action per screen.

Consent is a design element

Nothing about a person is shown — no photo, no precise location — until they choose to share it. Avatars use initials; the map shows regions, not addresses.

Abstract, not appropriated

Motifs (woven strands, the circle) are geometric abstractions of relationship and gathering. Final iconography must be reviewed with RTR’s Indigenous leadership.

Color

Drawn from the land: spruce forest, birchbark, prairie ochre, river water, wild berry. Spruce carries structure and action; ochre and river are the two relationship strands; berry is reserved for warnings.

Typography

Fraunces carries the voice — warm, human, a little old-growth. Atkinson Hyperlegible carries the reading — designed by the Braille Institute for maximum legibility, chosen because accessibility is a stated goal of this platform, not an afterthought.

Brand & motifs

The mark

Two arcs — ochre and river — form one circle around two people. The circle is open at top and bottom: the relationship is never closed to others.

The woven strand

Two strands interlacing — used as a section divider and under page titles. Use once per view, never as wallpaper.

Accessibility commitments

✓ Body face is Atkinson Hyperlegible, designed for low-vision readers.

✓ Text and interactive colors meet WCAG AA contrast on their backgrounds.

✓ Every interactive element shows a visible ochre focus ring.

✓ Touch targets are at least 44px; base text is 16.5px.

✓ prefers-reduced-motion disables all transitions.

✓ Status is never conveyed by color alone — pills carry text, seats carry counts.

✓ Layouts collapse to a single column below 620px; nav collapses at 860px.

✓ Plain language throughout; reading level checked with RTR staff.
