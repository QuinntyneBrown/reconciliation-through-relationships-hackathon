Design mocks

HTML mockups for the RTR Matching Portal, built from the challenge brief and product notes. Direction: “two strands, one circle” — spruce and birch from the land, ochre and river as the two relationship strands, and the cohort circle as the signature element. All names and data are synthetic.

Design system

Component libraryEvery component in one document — color, type, buttons, forms, tags, cards, cohort circle, navigation, alerts, tables, dialogs, empty states, accessibility commitments.

Pages

The primary end-to-end participant journey, then the facilitator and administrator views.

01LandingPublic welcome page — the invitation into the journey, four steps, participation categories. 02Sign inMagic-link authentication (lightweight auth per the hackathon scope), with sent-link confirmation state. 03OnboardingProfile intake, step 2 of 5 — basics, participation categories (required), faith tradition, bio. 04Learning journeyFour video modules with progress tracking, locked/complete states, eligibility explanation. 05Participant dashboardRecommendation waiting, discovery filters, cohort-readiness circle, events, messages, profile nudge. 06My profileView/edit own profile — location & treaty, faith & language, interests, availability, private boundaries, consent switches. 07Connection reviewAn approved recommendation — profile, “why you two,” facilitator note, accept/decline/ask. 08MessagesTwo-pane messaging between approved connections, boundaries banner, schedule-a-call entry point. 09Events & gatheringsCohort events including June 21 and September 30 national days, registration states, empty state. 10Regional map (facilitator)Schematic region map with cohort circles, privacy rules, region list with “form cohort” action. 11Facilitator overviewStats, match review queue, pending registrations, waitlist imbalance alert, reminders, safeguarding. 12AdministrationPeople & roles, learning content management, notification automations, cohort threshold, sign-in method.

Dialogs

Connection recommendationParticipant receives a facilitator-approved match — accept, see profile, or decide later. Schedule an introductory callTime slots highlighting mutual availability; video or phone; no-recording notice. Map consentOpt-in to the regional map — exactly what is and isn’t shown, changeable any time. Approve a match (facilitator)Side-by-side comparison, match factors, required personal note, approve/adjust/decline. Create an eventFacilitator event form with region-wide invitations and venue privacy. Waitlist pathwayKind handling of registration imbalance — waitlist or learning-resources pathway. Send reminders (facilitator)Recipient summary, template picker, editable preview, send-myself-a-test. Learning completeThe celebration moment — eligibility explained, next steps, consent entry point.

Sources

Cultural review

All motifs are deliberate geometric abstractions (woven strands, an open circle). Before launch, review iconography, language, and synthetic content with RTR’s Indigenous leadership and Joel Gordon.
