Reconciliation begins with a relationship.

RTR brings Indigenous and non-Indigenous people together to form relationships as Christians—learning the truth, listening well, and walking together in your own community. Not a program. A friendship rooted in shared faith.

The journey

Four steps, at your pace

1

Create your profile

Share who you are, what you value, and how you prefer to connect.

2

Complete the learning journey

Build a shared foundation for respectful, meaningful conversation.

3

Meet your match

You choose whether a facilitator matches you—every recommendation is reviewed, and introductions happen only when both people say yes.

4

Build the relationship

Talk, listen, and take part in reconciliation in your community.
