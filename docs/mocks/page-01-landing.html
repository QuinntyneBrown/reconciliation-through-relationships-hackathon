The journey

Four steps, at your pace

1

Tell us who you are

Create a profile — where you live, what you care about, the treaty territory nearest you, and how you like to connect.

2

Learn the truth first

A short learning journey with RTR’s videos prepares you before any introduction. Relationship starts with understanding.

3

Be introduced, with care

A facilitator personally reviews every match. Both of you choose — nothing happens without a yes from each side.

4

Gather locally

When five participants are ready in one region, a reconciliation cohort forms — film screenings, blanket exercises, shared meals.

Who it’s for

Every seat in the circle matters
